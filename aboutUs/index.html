We are committed to transforming healthcare accessibility for everyone.

Our Mission

"Queue Less, Care More!" is not just a slogan; it's our foundational promise.

Our mission at QueCare is to revolutionize patient experiences by significantly reducing wait times and streamlining healthcare services. We leverage innovative technology to connect patients with medical facilities efficiently, ensuring timely and compassionate care. We aim to empower patients with control over their healthcare journey and provide hospitals with tools to optimize their operations, leading to better outcomes for all.

Our Story

QueCare was founded in 2023 with a simple yet powerful vision: to eliminate the frustration of long queues and waiting times in healthcare. Observing the challenges faced by patients and medical staff alike, our founders envisioned a digital platform that could bring efficiency and transparency to the healthcare ecosystem.

From humble beginnings, developing our first live token tracker, we've grown into a comprehensive platform offering a suite of solutions from crowd monitoring to online consultations. Every step of our journey has been driven by a passion to make quality healthcare more accessible, convenient, and humane for millions.
