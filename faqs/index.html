Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

QueCare is an innovative healthcare management platform designed to streamline patient experiences by reducing wait times, optimizing hospital operations, and improving access to medical services through features like live token tracking, online appointments, second opinions, and more.

QueCare utilizes real-time crowd monitoring, live token tracking, and efficient appointment scheduling systems. This allows patients to monitor queue lengths remotely and hospitals to manage patient flow more effectively, significantly cutting down on physical waiting times.

Many core features of QueCare, such as general appointment booking and live token tracking, are free for patients. Premium features like advanced online consultations or specific medical report analysis might involve a fee, which will be clearly indicated.

Account & Booking

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" or "Register" button on our homepage. You'll need to provide some basic information like your name, email, and phone number, and create a secure password.

Log in to your QueCare account, navigate to the "Book Now" section or select a specific hospital/doctor. Choose your desired specialty, doctor (if applicable), date, and time slot. Confirm your details, and your appointment will be booked. You will receive a confirmation.

Services & Features

Crowd Monitoring provides real-time information about the number of patients currently in different departments or queues within a hospital. This helps patients avoid peak times and hospitals to manage their resources more effectively.

Our "Second Opinion" feature allows you to securely upload your medical reports and symptoms to receive a professional opinion from another specialist. This helps you make informed decisions about your health.

Yes, all online consultations on QueCare are conducted through secure, encrypted channels to ensure your privacy and data security. Our platform adheres to strict data protection standards.

Technical Support

On the login page, click on "Forgot Password?". You will be guided through a process to reset your password using your registered email address or phone number.

For any technical issues or support, please visit our "Contact Us" page or send an email to care@quecare.com. Our support team will assist you promptly.
